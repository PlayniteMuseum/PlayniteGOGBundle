Description

Nox Archaist is a brand-new Ultima-style CRPG from 6502 Workshop, endorsed by Richard Garriott. For Windows, MacOS, and original Apple II hardware, available as a Digital Download and Collector's Boxed Set. 

Within a deep and engaging story, you control one or more characters who travel Vali, visiting towns and castles, exploring dungeons, and fighting monsters. As your characters grow in wealth, experience and skill, they will face ever greater foes and challenges as you seek to save the world from an unknown and terrible danger.

Lord British himself has traveled the metaverse to Vali, to aid you on your quest!
