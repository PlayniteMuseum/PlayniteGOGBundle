Description

Experience an adventure full of surprises and save the kingdom from the evil wizard.

The pursuit of the evil wizard Fendrel has led you to an unknown kingdom. The local people live their peaceful lives here, unaware that the evil wizard has returned to their land and is plotting his terrible revenge. A darkness is coming that will engulf the whole kingdom. You can’t let that happen. You must seek out the forces of good and stop the evil wizard. You can count on fortune to favor those who are brave. An adventure full of surprises and heroic deeds awaits you.

Explore the kingdom and stop the evil wizard’s terrible revenge.
Help people and complete many interesting quests.
Fight monsters and learn many skills.
Find hundreds of useful hidden items.
Earn up to 26 achievements.

Play through the third and final episode of the Lost Tales storyline, which takes you right before the events of the original Hero of the Kingdom game. Enjoy a casual and lovely adventuring RPG that features classic story-driven Point&Click exploration in an old-school isometric style. Embark on a journey to explore a beautiful country, help people and complete many interesting quests. Learn skills, trade and collect items in your inventory. Earn nice rewards for your good deeds and achievements. Get ready for a new heroic adventure to stop the evil wizard’s terrible revenge.
